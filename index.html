About the cause

The City Youth Art Center has been a safe haven for over a decade, providing free after-school arts programming to teenagers in our community. From painting and sculpture to digital design and music production, we give kids the tools to express themselves, build confidence, and stay off the streets.

However, recent budget cuts have threatened our ability to keep our doors open five days a week. We are raising $25,000 to cover essential operating costs for the next six months, including rent, utilities, and art supplies.

Your support directly impacts the lives of over 500 teens who rely on our center. Whether you make a direct donation or purchase one of the amazing services offered by our community partners above, every dollar helps us ensure that creativity continues to thrive in our city.

"This center isn't just about art. It's about giving these kids a place where they belong and feel valued. Losing it would be devastating."

— MARCUS T., CENTER DIRECTOR
